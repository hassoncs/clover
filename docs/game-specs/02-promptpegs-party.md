## Executive Summary

**PromptPegs Party** transforms the classic Peggle formula into an AI-powered collectible experience. Players shoot balls through peg-filled boards to clear objectives, but the twist is **themed peg collections** generated by AI. Say "donut pegs in candy land" and watch as the game instantly creates matching sprites, backgrounds, and effects.

The core loop combines proven Peggle mechanics with Pokemon-style collection meta-progression: **Shoot â†’ Collect â†’ Unlock â†’ Share**. Players chase themed peg sets, unlock new visual styles, and share their wildest board combinations.

**Key Innovation**: AI theme generation turns every board into a unique visual experience while maintaining familiar, satisfying physics gameplay.

## 1. Detailed Game Mechanics

### 1.1 Core Peggle Mechanics (From Slopeggle)

**Aim & Shoot System**:
- Touch and drag to aim cannon (visual trajectory line)
- Release to fire ball with physics-accurate trajectory
- Ball bounces through peg field with realistic physics
- Limited balls per level (typically 10-15)

**Peg Types & Behaviors**:
- **Blue Pegs**: Standard pegs, award points when hit (10 Ã— multiplier)
- **Orange Pegs**: Target pegs, must clear all to win (100 Ã— multiplier)
- **Special Pegs**: Enhanced behaviors for themed collections

**Scoring System**:
- Hit multiplier increases with each peg hit in a single shot
- Multiplier resets between shots
- Bonus points for bucket catches (+500 + free ball)
- Chain reactions and combos for special peg types

**Physics Features**:
- Realistic ball physics with bounce, friction, gravity
- Proximity slow-motion when near orange pegs
- Camera shake and zoom effects for impact
- Portals for advanced level design

## 9. Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### 9.1 Technical Risks

**Asset Loading Performance**:
- **Risk**: Theme switching causes frame drops
- **Mitigation**: Preload themes, use asset caching, optimize image sizes

**Collection Data Corruption**:
- **Risk**: Lost collection progress frustrates players
- **Mitigation**: Implement backup/restore, validate data integrity, graceful degradation

**AI Generation Costs**:
- **Risk**: Theme generation becomes expensive at scale
- **Mitigation**: Cache generated themes, limit custom generations, optimize prompts

### 9.2 Design Risks

**Collection Grind**:
- **Risk**: Set completion feels tedious rather than rewarding
- **Mitigation**: Balanced peg spawn rates, meaningful unlock rewards, progress visibility

**Theme Fatigue**:
- **Risk**: Players lose interest in new themes
- **Mitigation**: Diverse theme styles, seasonal themes, community-driven content

**Complexity Creep**:
- **Risk**: Too many features overwhelm core Peggle experience
- **Mitigation**: Maintain simple core loop, optional complexity layers, user testing

### 9.3 Market Risks

**Peggle Saturation**:
- **Risk**: Market already has enough Peggle clones
- **Mitigation**: Focus on unique collection/AI angle, strong visual differentiation

**Collection Fatigue**:
- **Risk**: Players tired of collection mechanics
- **Mitigation**: Make collection optional, focus on gameplay first, meaningful rewards
